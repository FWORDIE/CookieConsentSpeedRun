Welcome!

Since GDPR came into our lives, we've all had to struggle with obtaining our basic privacy rights. Pop-up by pop-up we have all been honing our skills, learning to read ambiguous buttons and navigate Escher like sets of options. Now is your chance to show what you have learnt.

Your task is simple, press start, open your cookie settings, say no to all cookies and then save your settings. This game uses all the text and tricks of normal cookie consent pop-ups and banners, so don't blame me if it's too hard.
